HuxleyFM Help

Playing Music

You can choose a radio station to play by clicking the “Choose a SomaFM station” menu and clicking a station name.

You can also start typing a station name to filter the menu. Hit Enter to start playing the station at the top of the list. Hit Esc to close the station menu.

Chromecast

A radio station must be playing to be able to Chromecast. Click the Chromecast button cast in the top right to scan your network for Chromecast devices. Both Chromecast and Chromecast Audio devices will work. The Chromecast icon will turn into a spinning arrow refresh while devices are located.

Choose a Chromecast device from the list and HuxleyFM will connect to it. Music will stop playing from your computer and instead play via the Chromecast. To disconnect from Chromecast, click the filled-in Chromecast button cast_connected.
